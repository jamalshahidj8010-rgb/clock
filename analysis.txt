Digital Clock Project Requirements

1. Project Overview
Decide on form factor: physical clock versus software/app

Define working title or model name

Identify primary user needs or pain points (readability, integration, world-time)

2. Business Objectives
List top three goals (e.g., unit sales target, app downloads, platform integrations)

Determine success metrics (sales numbers, active users, session time, CSAT)

3. Scope and Deliverables
Specify core features for MVP (12h/24h display, alarms, snooze, timers, weather/calendar modules)

Clarify what’s out of scope at launch

Outline deliverables: hardware prototype, firmware, mobile/​web app, user manual, packaging
